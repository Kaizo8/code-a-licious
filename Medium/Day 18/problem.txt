You start at position 0 on a never-ending line at time 0.
Each time period, you can either stay put or move a specific distance to the left or right.
This distance depends on the current time. For example, if you're at position d at time i-1, at time i you can be at position d-i, d, or d+i.
Your goal is to figure out the earliest moment when you reach to position x.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Input:
The first and only line of input contains an integer x - Your destination point.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Output:
Print the earliest possible time for you to reach the coordinate x.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Time Limit: 2 sec
Memory Limit: 256 MB
