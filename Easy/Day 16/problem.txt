Imagine a scenario where you're tasked with procuring a specific quantity of pens from a store.
This store offers two purchasing options: one pen for x rupees or three pens for y rupees. 
Your goal is to determine the minimum amount of money required to obtain exactly n pens. 

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Input:
The first and only line of input contains three integers x, y (1 <= x, y <= 100), n (1 <[= n <= 100]) - cost for 1 pen, cost for 3 pens, and total number of pens you require.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Output:
Print a single integer - minimum cost to buy exactly n pens.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Time Limit: 2 sec.
Memory Limit: 1024 MB.
