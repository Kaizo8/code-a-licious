You are given a rectangle in the xy-plane. 
Each edge of this rectangle is parallel to the x- or y-axis.
Given the coordinates of three of the four vertices of this rectangle, (x1 ,y1), (x2 ,y2), and (x3 ,y3).
Your task is to calculate the fourth vertex.
NOTE: Area of the rectangle is not zero.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Input:
Input contains three vertices - (x1 ,y1), (x2 ,y2), and (x3 ,y3).

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Output:
Find the fourth coordinate.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Time Limit: 2 sec
Memory Limit: 1024 MB
